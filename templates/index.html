PRACTICE DOES NOT ALWAYS MAKE PERFECT!

The purpose of this project is to determine factors beyond training hard that influence ultimate high school cross country race performance. I was generously provided anonymous race and training log data from XCStats () from 2011 through the beginning of the 2019 cross country season.

Visualizations

Weather elements plotted versus latitude included temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.
